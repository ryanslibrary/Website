How much does an EICR cost in Kent?

EICR costs depend on property size. For a typical 3-bedroom home in Sevenoaks, Tonbridge or Tunbridge Wells, expect to pay between £150–£250. Contact us for a free quote →

How long does a full house rewire take?

A typical 3-bedroom house rewire takes 5–8 days. We'll give you a clear schedule before work begins and keep disruption to a minimum throughout.

Do I need planning permission for an EV charger?

For most Kent homes, no. Recent changes to permitted development rules mean most residential EV charger installations no longer need planning permission. Listed buildings and conservation areas may still require it — we'll advise before starting.

Are you registered and accredited?

Yes. Burleigh Electrical Services Ltd is registered with a government-approved scheme. All our work is certified and compliant with BS 7671 wiring regulations, and all notifiable work is registered with your local authority.

Do you provide a certificate after the work?

Yes. You'll receive the appropriate certificate for every job — an Electrical Installation Certificate (EIC) for new installations, or a Minor Electrical Installation Works Certificate for smaller jobs. All Part P notifiable work is registered.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

We offer emergency call-outs across Kent. Call us directly on 07886 067085 for the fastest response.

What areas do you cover?

We cover all of West Kent — Sevenoaks, Tunbridge Wells, Tonbridge, Westerham, Hildenborough, Borough Green, Edenbridge, Hadlow, Paddock Wood, Otford, Swanley and all surrounding villages.
